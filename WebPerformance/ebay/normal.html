About Nikon D90

If you are into DSLR cameras, you know that Nikon is an absolute powerhouse when it comes to image quality, camera control, and top-of-the-line lenses. The Nikon D90 is no exception. It boasts a very pleasing 12.3-megapixel sensor and some of the best glass you can find in a consumer camera. The Nikon D90 captures up to 4.5 images per second, uses an accurate 11-point auto-focus system, and functions at top speed. You can buy a Nikon D90 body, or you can opt for the Nikon D90 kit, which comes with an 18-105mm lens. This lens definitely makes the kit worthwhile unless you already own a similar lens. With a massive focal length that more than doubles the standard 18-55mm lens, the lens is likely to become your standing favorite. You can find both the cameras and kits on eBay sold by numerous reliable sellers. If you are looking for a great all-around DSLR, this Nikon model is a great choice.
