Nike manufactures more than athletic shoes for men and women. The leading sports garment manufacturer also produces a successful line of Nike jackets that you can find by shopping on eBay. Look stylish as you glide through the streets wearing your comfortable and moisture-resistant Nike running jacket. The jacket does an excellent job of repelling moisture, and outdoorsmen wear the coat on damp outings. You can wear the Nike winter jacket during the coldest nights of the year.
