About Android Unlocked

Apple once reigned as the undisputed king of the smartphone world, but that is no longer the case, as of 2013. Google's Android phones made up almost four out of every five smartphones available on the market in 2013, and Android unlocked phones sell extremely well in both new and used condition. An unlocked phone is particularly useful for users who do not want to commit to long-term usage with a particular mobile carrier. An unlocked Android 3G phone takes full advantage of the user's 3G network of choice in the continental United States and abroad. An unlocked Android 2.2 phone offers a user the opportunity to choose a low-cost deal on a slightly older model that still offers great features on the Android platform. The latest models are also available with home screen tips widgets to assist new users, greatly improved security, improved browser performance, and more.
