Top 10 Air Max Shoes
February 15, 2015 . Views . Comments Comment . 3 Vote 3 Votes
Please sign in to vote on this Guide.
Nike's Air Max shoes are a perfect fit for any athlete. Designed by the same man who created the Air Jordan line of sneakers, these shoes keep wearers comfortable while they are active. They utilize a special air cushion that provides a unique level of comfort. Whether wearers plan on running, playing basketball, or just going to the gym, these shoes keep their feet feeling fine. Buyers can find a wide selection of Air Max shoes on the market, so it may be difficult to pick just one pair. The following list of some of the best Air Max shoes available should help buyers find what they seek.

1
Nike Air Max 2015

This modern iteration of the Air Max brand has more than enough to offer the athletic shoe connoisseur. The flexible cushioning and supportive fit allow for long walks or running sessions with little to no fatigue. The sculpted cushion midsole is responsible for much of this comfort, as it adds soft feedback in response to any hard impact.

2
Nike Air Max 1 Mid Waterproof

These shoes protect against both impact and the elements. The leather outer layer repels water, while the Max Air unit adds protection from impact. Other features, such as the flex groove, facilitate more natural foot movement. The Nike Air Max 1 Mid Waterproof is an excellent choice for women in search of a sneaker/boot for their active lifestyle.

3
Nike Air Max Lunar 90

Nike Air Max Lunar 90 shoes come in a variety of colorful styles such as metallic silver, anthracite, black, and volt. Additionally, the pattern on the outsole allows for additional durability and traction. These shoes feature a Lunarlon midsole that works with the Air-Sole unit to provide maximum comfort.

4
Nike Air Max 1 Mid Premium Pendleton iD

On the inside, these shoes have everything that goes into a Nike Air Max sneaker, but the exterior is wool. Pendleton Woolen Mills helped design these shoes, and as a result they are available in four solid colors and plaid. Users can even pair this design with leather if they see fit.

5
Nike Air Max 1 Mid Sneaker Boot

This stylish footwear combines the physical attributes of both sneakers and boots to create something unique and comfortable. The waterproof leather prevents uncomfortable wetness, and the rubber outsole reduces slipping to an absolute minimum. The Nike Air Max 1 Mid Sneaker Boots are great for jogging anywhere.

6
Women's Air Max 90

Women's Air Max 90 shoes are ideal for any woman who wants to go jogging and get active. They offer supreme comfort, thanks to the leather and mesh upper with supportive overlays, and the Waffle outsole prevents any slipping. Additionally, these shoes come in a wide variety of colors and styles.

7
Nike Fingertrap Max

The Fingertrap Max is one pair of shoes that is sure to attract positive attention. The unique, multicolored, woven exterior provides an additional level of breathability. Aside from this, all of the great Air Max features are here, such as the midsole cushioning and excellent flexibility. Anyone who wants extra comfort while looking great in the process should get these shoes.

8
Nike Air Max Lunar1 Deluxe

Like most Nike products, Lunar1 Deluxe shoes have an unmistakable style. The leather and safari print in multiple colors grace the shoe's exterior. Best of all, there is no sacrifice in quality, as the ultra-cushioned Air-Sole unit and rubber outsole are all here.

9
Nike Air Max Thea

The Thea shoe is smaller than many of its contemporaries, and the design is a bit minimalist, as well. That said, it still offers everything that one would come to expect in an Air Max product. The Air-Sole and Phylon sole tuck neatly inside this subtle shoe.

10
Nike Air Max 90 Premium iD

Anyone who wants to stand out with a great shoe should consider picking a pair of Nike's Premium iD sneakers. The embossed leather exterior exudes style, and the shoes are available in a variety of color combinations. As always, the cushioned sole is inside and offers great protection while running.
